Common Questions About Our Dumpster Rentals

How long can I keep the dumpster?

Our standard rental period is 7 days, but we offer flexible terms. Need it longer? Just ask—we’ll work with your timeline.

What can I throw in the dumpster?

We accept most construction debris, household junk, yard waste, furniture, appliances (without Freon), and more. We’ll go over what’s allowed and what’s restricted during your booking.

Do I need a permit?

In most residential areas, no permit is needed if the dumpster is on private property. If you plan to place it on the street, let us know and we’ll help with city permit requirements if needed.

What if I fill the dumpster before I’m done?

We can arrange a swap-out—just call us when it’s full, and we’ll replace it with an empty one so your project doesn’t miss a beat.
